Step 1 · Raw Material

The Yulin Cashmere Farm

In the transition zone between the Mu Us Desert and the Loess Plateau — Yulin, Shaanxi Province — our cashmere goats graze at 1,200 meters above sea level. The arid, cold climate and mineral-rich pasture create the ideal conditions for producing some of the finest cashmere fiber in the world.

We raise the Shaanxi Northern White Cashmere Goat, a breed refined over 25 years of selective breeding. Their undercoat produces fiber with a diameter of just 14.5 microns or less — putting our raw cashmere in the "ultra-fine" category, comparable to the most exclusive cashmere on the global market.

The Breed

Shaanxi Northern White Cashmere Goat

This breed is the result of decades of genetic improvement — crossing Liaoning cashmere goats with local Shaanxi black goats to create an animal that thrives in the harsh northern climate while producing exceptional fiber.

Why this matters for your garments:

Cashmere from goats raised in colder, drier climates produces a denser, finer undercoat. The Yulin region's winter temperatures regularly drop below -20 C, triggering the goats to develop an exceptionally warm, fine undercoat. When harvested each spring, this fiber is longer, softer, and more resilient than cashmere from milder regions.

The breed is also known for its disease resistance and low-stress temperament — factors that directly affect fiber quality. Stressed animals produce coarser, more brittle hair.

End-to-End Integration

From Pasture to Finished Garment

Six stages. One company. Zero middlemen.

1. Raw Material — Our Farm

Cashmere goats raised on our Yulin farm. Annual spring harvest of premium ultra-fine fiber (14.5μm). Direct quality control at the source.

2. Fiber Processing — Partner Mills

Raw cashmere is washed, de-haired, and spun into yarn at specialized mills in the Shaanxi-Ningxia textile corridor, under our quality supervision.

3. Authorized Fabric Agency — 200+ Premium Mills

As an authorized agent for European and American luxury fabric mills, we offer direct access to Loro Piana, NISHIOKA Orimono, Holland & Sherry, Drago, Milano Hemp, Egyptian cotton, and 200+ other mills — at agent pricing, with no trader markups.

4. Pattern & Design — In-House Studio

72-hour design proposals. Digital pattern development with multi-size grading. Your sketch becomes a technical production file ready for the cutting table.

5. Manufacturing — Our Factory

3-day sampling, 15-20 day bulk production. 10-point quality inspection at every stage. Self-owned factory means no outsourcing delays or quality drift.

6. Global DDP Delivery — To Your Door

Customs clearance, duties, freight, and last-mile delivery — all handled by us. Fixed price, zero surprises. 7-10 days to Europe, 10-14 days to North America.
