About HARCHOY

Self-Owned Factory. Direct Supply. Total Control.

HARCHOY (合裁) is a premier menswear and womenswear manufacturer specializing in OEM, ODM, and OBM bespoke apparel. We operate our own production facility, giving us complete control over every stage — from fabric sourcing to finished garment quality.

Our professional team provides 1-on-1 design service, meeting the personalized needs of tailoring studios worldwide and elevating corporate team images through custom attire. With a 5-star rating on Alibaba International and clients across 50+ countries, we've earned our reputation as a reliable, quality-first manufacturing partner.
